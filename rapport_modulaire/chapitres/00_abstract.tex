\chapter*{Abstract}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Abstract}

This final-year project was carried out within the Business Development Division of
Atlantic Re, an international reinsurer and subsidiary of the CDG Group, as part of
an initial assignment focused on strategic modelling of African expansion by 2030.
The primary objective was to build a prioritisation model for African Life and
Non-Life markets, based on a multi-criteria data science pipeline.

During the business immersion phase, we identified a structural problem that was
outside the original scope: the absence of a tool for consulting and exploiting
internal data, which made it difficult for the department's teams to analyse existing
portfolios and, consequently, compromised the reliability of the external model we
were building. In response to this finding, we proposed extending our mission to
include digitalisation, by developing a digital ecosystem serving as an internal
data governance portal.

The project therefore covers two complementary pillars: first, the audit of the
business database and the development of the digital ecosystem; second, the
construction of the data science pipeline (multi-source collection, hierarchical ML
imputation, SCAR multi-criteria scoring) leading to an operational ranking of African
markets for Atlantic Re.

\textbf{Keywords:} reinsurance, Africa, data science, machine learning, multi-criteria
scoring, data governance, digitalisation, strategic expansion.
